Service Cases

In this section we will show you three typical business cases of simple solutions we developed for our business partners. THE SITUATION:

During COVID period tourist activity stpped, an owner of a taxi company in Rome asked us to assess a potential business investment idea that would help them to keep his taxi-cab drivers crew busy and get by the period of turblence.

ACTIONS:

Based on partners agreement we developed a payback model, found critical factors of success, and agreed on major management rules to achieve result.

THE SITUATION:

Ukrainian bags manufacturing company had 2 critical issues. From one side the owners wanted to implement new approach to process management to increase productivity. Another was more critical - they had serious intrnal miscommunication issues that caused disruption in process.

ACTIONS:

We made a short 1-week analysis: reviewed technological process, observed how management decisions are made executed daily, brainstormed ideas with 2 owners and developed recomendations report with clear change actions. Because we did not implement anything it was done free of charge.
